Introduction to Wheel Throwing

A beginner-friendly workshop covering the fundamentals of the potter's wheel. Students will learn centering, pulling, and shaping clay into functional forms. No prior experience required.

Architectural Ceramics

An intermediate workshop focused on hand-building techniques for architectural applications — tiles, wall pieces, and sculptural installations. Explore form, texture, and glaze.
