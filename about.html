Rayne Milner

I've studied ceramics since 2017. I first learned in Wilmington, NC, my hometown, before moving to LA to practice. I continued my practice in San Francisco in the Mission district, where I now work.

I'm inspired by natural forms, and that comes through in my vessels and lighting.
